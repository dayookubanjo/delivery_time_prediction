Delivery Time Prediction (Logistics Company)

Problem Statement: A logistics company based in Lagos, Nigeria has been experiencing consistent delays in customer deliveries due to the increase in traffic in the state. This has caused the company's customer satisfaction score to decline drastically.

To solve this problem, i have developed a machine learning model to accurately predict and inform the customer on how long it will take in minutes for a rider to deliver a package using the pickup monthday, pickup weekday, pickup time (hour, minute and second), temperature in degree celsius, precipitation in milimeters, pickup location (latitude and longitude) and delivery location (latitude and longitude.
